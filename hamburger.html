The Greatest Hamburger Ever

The best burger recipe for summertime grilling! This juicy burger is jam-packed with all kinds of stuff and has no tasteless bread crumbs. Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs lean ground beef
  • 1.5 onion, finely chopped
  • 1.5 cup Cheddar cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 (1 ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1.5 teaspoon crushed dried rosemary
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat grill for high heat
  2. Mix ingredients into 4 patties
  3. Cook patties until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear (4-5 minutes per side)
